Sound and Lighting Services

Dopamine Events supplies Funktion One sound systems for various types of events and parties where superior amplified sound is required. Our goal is to enhance the overall experience and create memorable moments by delivering a seamless sonic performance. We also provide waterproof lighting to enhance ambiance and elevate the energy.

Experience and Expertise

The company collaborates with team members who possess extensive experience in the industry, including sound and lighting engineers. This expertise guarantees a high level of professionalism and meticulous attention to detail. Our objective is to ensure the seamless execution of events, from the initial stages of production to the final wrap-up.

Location

Although our headquarters are located in the South West near Gloucester, Dopamine Events serves clients and events across the entire U.K., encompassing nightclubs, bars, wedding venues, promoters, and festivals.

Sound Assessment and Noise Prediction

Dopamine Events provides comprehensive sound assessment services, utilising advanced noise prediction software to calculate and anticipate offsite noise pollution. Our dedicated team analyses the unique requirements and budget constraints of each venue, allowing us to recommend and implement the optimal equipment tailored to your specific needs.

Equipment Rental

The company boasts an extensive inventory of DJ equipment available for rent, featuring top-tier offerings such as Funktion One speakers, psm318 monitors, FFA amplifiers, and a variety of additional gear. We are able to facilitate multi-stage setups, complete with a comprehensive range of amplification and processing equipment

Lighting Services

Dopamine Events specialises in a wide range of lighting solutions, from moving heads and beams to wash lights and waterproof up-lighters. We excel in crafting captivating visual effects with Laserworld lasers and Pangolin software.

Safety and Insurance

All equipment rentals are subject to a deposit and contract. The company has public liability insurance and conducts PAT testing on all equipment to ensure safety and compliance with regulations.
